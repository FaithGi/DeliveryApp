ABOUT US

Books Of Bliss was established in 2001.Our company has had a tremendous impact on the South African Tertiary Education Market since its inception. Headed up by University graduates. Books Of Bliss prides itself on service excellence,cutting edge information and products. Books Of Bliss network is extensive , our commercial partnerships allow us to deliver material throughout Southern Africa on time and at the right price.We cater to the book needs of an extensive student and academic market. Book Of Bliss has a level of 1 BBBEE status.

We are official suppliers to the following institutions/libraries:
University of kwazulu-natal library(Westville ,medical,pietermaritzburg and Edgewood campuses)
University of Johannesburg (All campuses)
University of Zululand library
University of Free State library
University of western cape library
Durban University of Technology (all campuses)
Tshwane University of Technology
University of Mpumalanga
UNISA official Bookseller
